# Welcome to Memento Academy

> "Your fate if you're weary of information and what you really crave is connection."

Memento Academy is a learning and development ecosystem focused on today's most disruptive technologies. We are passionate about building bridges between complex information and human connection through technology.

## About us
We are an organization dedicated to training and developing innovative solutions in the **Web3**, **Blockchain**, and **Intelligent Systems** space. We believe in the power of decentralization (especially within the **IOTA** ecosystem) and how data can improve people's lives when managed with purpose.

## Featured Projects
Our repositories reflect our mission to innovate and educate:
- **Smart Meter Dashboard**: Advanced real-time energy consumption visualization using Next.js and Tailwind CSS.
- **IOTA NFT Marketplace**: A platform for minting and trading NFTs with a focus on efficiency and user experience.
- **zkLogin Integration**: Implementation of secure, gasless authentication to facilitate mass Web3 adoption.

## How to contribute
We love receiving collaborations! If you want to help us improve our projects or propose new ideas:
1. Explore our technical repositories.
2. Review the contribution guidelines in each project.
3. Open an Issue or submit a Pull Request.
